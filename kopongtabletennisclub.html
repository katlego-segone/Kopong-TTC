Welcome to Kopong Table Tennis Club

Affiliated with the Botswana Table Tennis Association, we empower the next generation through the love of table tennis.

About Us

Kopong Table Tennis Club is located in the heart of Kopong and is dedicated to nurturing talent from grassroots level, especially focusing on the youth from local primary schools. We offer a welcoming and vibrant space for young athletes to grow in discipline, skill, and confidence through the sport of table tennis.
